What is Depression?

Depression is a mental health condition where one feels the constant feeling of sadness and lost of interest in activities they used to enjoy. It can lead to many emotional and physical issues which decreases ones capability to perform their daily activities.

In general, depression occurs from a mix of events and factors. This results in different symptoms of depression from minor to severe. Thankfully, depression is treatable.

Warning Signs of Depression

Emotionally Physically
Sad, anxious or low mood Loss of appetite or over-eating
Low self esteem, guilt Trouble sleeping
Hopelessness Decreased energy
Suicidal thoughts Increased tearfulness
Lost of interest and motivation
Poor concentration

Concerned you or someone you know might have depression?

If you have taken note of the warning signs of depression and is concerned you or someone you love might have depression, please do provide help or seek for help and support from someone you trust or a professional. This website will provide you with resources to help you during this difficult time.
